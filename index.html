What is this?

RamTIC Online: Navigating the currents of knowledge. Empowering educators and students alike, this website serves as a dynamic hub for managing and understanding the intricacies of our classroom trout ecosystem. Dive into real-time data, explore interactive visualizations, and join us on this educational journey as we foster a deeper connection with nature through technology and hands-on learning.
